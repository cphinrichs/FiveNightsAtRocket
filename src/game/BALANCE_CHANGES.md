# Game Balance Changes - Difficulty Adjustment

## Summary of Changes

The game has been rebalanced to provide a fairer, more strategic experience that prevents spawn-killing and gives players time to learn the mechanics.

## Enemy Behavior Changes

### 1. **Activation Delays** (No More Spawn Kills!)
- **Jo-nathan**: 5-second delay before starting to chase
- **Jeromathy**: 8-second delay before starting patrol
- **Angellica**: 10-second delay before becoming active

This gives players ~5-10 seconds at the start of each day to:
- Orient themselves
- Plan their route
- Get to the break room safely
- Collect initial resources

- Provides a 10-second safe window

### 5. **Visual Feedback Improvements**

- **Red eyes** = Chasing (DANGER!)

**State Indicators (top-right corner of enemy):**
- **Red dot** = Chasing
- **Green dot** = Eating or at desk
- **Yellow dot** = Idle

## Strategic Gameplay Improvements

### Opening Strategy (First ~10 seconds)
1. You have 5-10 seconds before enemies activate
2. Go directly to Break Room
3. Grab egg from cabinet
4. Stock up on snacks from refrigerator
5. Return to safe area

### Mid-Game Strategy
- Track enemy desk timers
- Use cameras when enemies are at desks
- Feed Jo-nathan early for 10-second reprieve
- Keep 2-3 snacks in inventory (buffer)
- Watch YouTube only when Angellica is at her desk

### Resource Management
- **Eggs**: Use proactively, not reactively
- **Snacks**: Keep buffer of 2-3 to prevent panic
- **Battery**: Use cameras during enemy idle times

## Difficulty Progression

The game now has a natural difficulty curve:

**Early Game (Minutes 0-2):**
- Enemies inactive or at desks
- Safe resource collection
- Learning phase

**Mid Game (Minutes 2-5):**
- Enemies begin patrolling
- Need to track positions
- Resource management critical

**Late Game (Minutes 5+):**
- More aggressive behavior
- Tighter resource margins
- Strategic camera use essential

   - Keep egg in inventory
   - Keep 2-3 snacks minimum
   - Restock when safe

3. **Enemy Management:**
   - Feed Jo-nathan early for breathing room
   - Watch for Jeromathy at his desk
   - Check cameras when Angellica is at desk
